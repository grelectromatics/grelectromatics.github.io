What is MPPT?

MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracking) technology dynamically adjusts voltage and current to ensure solar panels operate at maximum efficiency under varying sunlight conditions.

MPPT vs PWM Controllers

MPPT controllers are significantly more efficient than PWM controllers, especially in low-light conditions, making them ideal for modern solar systems.
